Charito is a futuristic squarish sans-serif font. Support seven scripts - Latin, Greek, Cyrillic, Armenian, Georgian, Hebrew and Arabic.

This font (Charito) is free alternative to paid fonts - Hyperspace Race Extended (Swell Type), Tactic Sans Extra Extended (Miller Type Foundry), Manifold Extended CF (Connary Fagen Type Design).
